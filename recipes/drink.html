Strawberry Smoothie

Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Add strawberries
  2. Add banana
  3. Add yogurt
  4. Add pineapple juice
  5. Add sugar
  6. Add milk; then blend together in a Blender
